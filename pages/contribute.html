Interested in Contributing to HospitalRun?

Currently, there are a few ways to contribute. As the project grows, they’ll be more. We hope you catch the vision for what we’re doing together and get involved in whatever way you can.

Become a Code Contributor

HospitalRun is proudly built with technologies like Node, React, Fastify and CouchDB. If you work with those technologies already or have software development experience and are interested in learning them, consider contributing to HospitalRun. If you’re serious about contributing, we also encourage you to join our Slack, where the majority of the project communication happens.

Contribute
Make a financial contribution to support the development and deployment of HospitalRun

Contribute on our Open Collective page! and thank you for helping support some of the costs around the project.

If you’d like to make a special financial contribution to support the project, email us at donations@hospitalrun.io to discuss how you can assist.
